The heart of SnapMind is the CLI. Type `snapmind` from anywhere in your root directory to invoke an AI powered by your local codebase, reading hundreds of files directly via LanceDB.

~/projects/acme-corp
# Start the CLI with the 'coder' persona mapped to your folder
$snapmind --persona coder --mount .

Commands & Personas

Coder

Your programming buddy. Understands TypeScript, Python, Go. Can generate patches, explain complex architectures, and review git diffs.

$ snapmind --persona coder

Scholar

Built for reading papers. Automatically parses local `.pdf` files, indexing chapters. Extract conclusions and cross-reference citations.

$ snapmind --persona scholar

Analyst

Consumes CSV, JSON, and log files. Can write python scripts in a sandbox to plot graphs directly from the terminal or aggregate datasets.

$ snapmind --persona analyst
